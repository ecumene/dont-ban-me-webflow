TrainTrack

TrainTrack is a platform for sharing skills in your local area.

Why TrainTrack?

Are applicants hard to come by?

Penta and Starry are patiently waiting for new people to work with.

What if you shared your skills?

Instead of waiting for the job market to cool down, or amazing people to fall in your lap. Why not create a local skill-share?

Create a personalized curriculum.

Students are taught in a 1-1 tutor program. This is to ensure they build a broad network, and set of soft skills.


This also means students and coaches can continue their jobs or school as they complete your program.

Organize mono-e-mono learning.

Students interact with more than one coach in the program. This gives them a diverse skill set, and they only change coaches when they've learned that skill.

Immediately Hireable new-professionals.

Using this method, anyone can learn the skills they need to be successful, and build a network of like-minded fellows in the industry who can help them get hired.

Maybe some of those fresh graduates would fit well at your job?
